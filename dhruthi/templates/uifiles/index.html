About

Dhruthi Health Care

Dhruthi Health Care is a medical center that offers clinical and hospital care. Ranked among the top hospitals in Guntur, we provide specialized medical care to thousands of patients every year. We offer advanced technology, the latest treatments and are among the safest places in healthcare today. Our renowned medical experts work as a team to deliver comprehensive care across a broad range of specialties, and we always put our patients at the center of everything we do.

Our healthcare system includes hospitals, outpatient clinics and wellness centers in three major locations in and around Guntur District, with state-of-the-art facilities in the following locations – Lakshmipuram and Kothapet in Guntur and health care center in Chirala.

Whether you’re seeking a diagnosis, a second opinion, or beyond, our medical representatives are here to help answer your questions and support you every step of the way. For more information or to speak with our local representative, please contact us on our helpline number

Meet Ur Doctors

Meet Dr. Revuri Hari Krishna

M.D (General Medicine)

Dr. Revuri Hari Krishna is an extremely committed and passionate doctor in General Medicine. After completing post graduation and MD (internal medicine), he started his career as a faculty in NRI medical college. He is proficient in evaluating and treating infectious as well as chronic issues such as hypertension, diabetes and thyroid abnormalities. He is one of the best general medicine doctors in Guntur, India and he is an active participant in various national and international medical societies and also in academic research activities. Dr. Harikrishna has established Dhruthi Hospitals one of the best general hospital in guntur which is providing best general medicine in Guntur City with its core principle as patient welfare and quick recovery. He has extensive experience of 10-15 years as a renowned doctor and has the capacity to evaluate root cause of the patient problems. He is an exceptionally compassionate doctor and is available 24/7 for patient welfare

Meet Dr. Lakshmi Nimmaraju

M.D (General Medicine)

Dr. Lakshmi Nimmaraju has special care for Dental Implant treatments. She did her BDS from SIBAR Institute of dental sciences, Guntur in 2006. She completed her MDS from SIBAR Institute of dental sciences, Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, Guntur in 2012. She presented paper at 15th IOS PG Students convention at Manali in Himachal Pradesh, March 2011. She ensures that her patients have a full range of latest options available in her specialization. She handles the most complicated cases with utmost ease and stands as best dental doctor in Guntur, AP.
